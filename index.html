Responsive.

Bolt is responsive meaning it looks great on all devices. It automatically adjusts to the browser size so everything is readable on the smallest mobile phone to the largest desktop computer.

Crisp Graphics.

This theme is ready for high resolution displays on all kinds of retina display devices. Everything will look crisp and clean no matter which device you are browsing with.

Customizable.

Bolt is split up into easily customizable sections that you can mix and match to make what fits your needs. Bolt comes with multiple layouts, but think of these as just suggestions. You can easily make your own layout that works perfectly for you.
